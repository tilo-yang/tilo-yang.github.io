Dr. Tilo H. Yang is a Postdoctoral Associate at the Research Laboratory of Electronics, MIT, working with Prof. Jing Kong. His research focuses on the integration of ultrawide-bandgap materials with 2D materials, nanodevice synthesis, and microelectronic packaging for next-generation nanoelectronics. His expertise spans electron microscopy, device fabrication, and heterogeneous integration.
