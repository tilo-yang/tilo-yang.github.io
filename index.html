Dr. Tilo H. Yang is a Postdoctoral Associate at the Research Laboratory of Electronics (RLE), MIT, working with Prof. Jing Kong. His research focuses on integration of ultrawide-bandgap materials with 2D materials, nanodevice synthesis, and heterogeneous microelectronic packaging for next-generation nanoelectronics.

Email ORCID Google Scholar ResearchGate LinkedIn

Location: 77 Massachusetts Avenue, Cambridge, MA

Research

Research interests include low-dimensional materials, nanoelectronic devices, microelectronic packaging, scanning transmission electron microscopy, and solid thermodynamics/phase equilibria.

Current Projects

  • Integration of ultrawide-bandgap materials with 2D materials for nanoelectronics
  • Ferroelectric switching in rhombohedral-stacked MoS2
  • Defect engineering and optoelectronic characterization of 2D materials

Technical Expertise

  • Scanning & transmission electron microscopy (STEM, SEM, TEM)
  • E-beam lithography, device fabrication, ion milling
  • Raman/PL spectroscopy and electrical characterization
  • Microelectronic packaging / heterogeneous integration
