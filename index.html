Dr. Tilo H. Yang is a Postdoctoral Associate at the Research Laboratory of Electronics (RLE), Massachusetts Institute of Technology, working with Prof. Jing Kong. His research focuses on integrating ultrawide-bandgap and 2D materials for nanoelectronic devices, exploring ferroelectric switching phenomena, and advancing heterogeneous microelectronic packaging for next-generation integrated systems.
